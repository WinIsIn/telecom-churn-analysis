## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, KiwiTel should prioritise the following retention strategies:

1. **Contract Upgrade Campaigns** — Target month-to-month customers in months 3-11 with incentives to switch to annual contracts. Expected churn reduction: ~15-20% in this segment.

2. **Proactive Support Escalation** — Automatically flag accounts with 3+ support calls for a proactive outreach call from a retention specialist before the customer self-churns.

3. **Fiber Optic Loyalty Pricing** — Fiber customers are price-sensitive and face strong competitor pressure. A 10% loyalty discount at the 6-month mark for high-value fiber customers would protect the highest-revenue cohort.

4. **New Customer Onboarding Programme** — Customers with tenure < 12 months and month-to-month contracts are the highest churn risk. A structured 90-day onboarding programme with check-in calls at days 30, 60, and 90 would improve early retention.

5. **Payment Friction Reduction** — Electronic check payers have higher late payment rates. Incentivise auto-pay (bank transfer / credit card) with a small monthly discount to reduce late payments and associated churn signals.
